About RhythmDo

Built around how you actually work.

RhythmDo started as a personal tool to stop losing track of what to work on next. It became an app for anyone who wants to build a consistent focus habit — without complicated systems.

Our mission

Focus isn’t about doing more — it’s about doing what matters, consistently.

Most productivity apps force one workflow on everyone. RhythmDo respects that people work differently. That’s why the app supports three working styles — each with its own home screen priorities and rhythm.

Finisher

Loves closing tabs and clearing lists. Quick wins first, momentum builds naturally.

Deep Worker

Thrives in long, uninterrupted blocks. Fewer sessions, higher intensity.

Consistency Builder

Wins by showing up every day. Streaks, small habits, steady progress.
