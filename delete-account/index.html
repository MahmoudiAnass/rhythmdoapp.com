Account & data deletion

Request Account & Data Deletion

RhythmDo

If you have signed in to RhythmDo using your Google account and wish to have your account and associated data deleted, please submit a request using the form below.

What gets deleted

  • ✓ Your Firebase Authentication account (sign-in identity)
  • ✓ Any Firebase-linked usage analytics associated with your user ID

What may be retained (and for how long)

  • • Anonymized crash reports (Crashlytics): retained up to 90 days per Google Firebase’s standard data retention policy. These cannot be linked back to your identity after deletion.
  • • AdMob advertising interaction data: handled by Google’s advertising infrastructure. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
Note: All task data, sessions, badges, and preferences are stored locally on your device only and are automatically removed when you uninstall the app. They are not stored on our servers.
How to request deletion

Steps to delete your RhythmDo account

Follow these steps to have your account and associated data removed.

  1. 1
    Fill in the form below

    Enter the email address you used to sign in to RhythmDo with your Google account, then submit the form.

  2. 2
    Or email us directly

    Send an email to contact@rhythmdoapp.com with the subject “Account Deletion Request” from the address associated with your account.

  3. 3
    We verify and process your request

    Within 30 days, we will delete your Firebase Authentication account and linked usage analytics, then send a confirmation email.

  4. 4
    Uninstall the app (optional)

    Because tasks, sessions and badges live on your device, uninstalling RhythmDo removes any remaining local data.

Data type Action Retention
Firebase Authentication account Deleted Within 30 days
Firebase-linked usage analytics Deleted Within 30 days
Tasks, sessions, badges, preferences Deleted On app uninstall (stored locally)
Anonymized crash reports (Crashlytics) Retained Up to 90 days (cannot be linked to you)
AdMob advertising interaction data Retained by Google Per Google’s Privacy Policy
